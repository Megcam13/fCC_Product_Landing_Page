About Meg's Herbal Market

Meg's herbs are locally grown, using only 100% organic soil & ingredients, no harsh chemicals or solutions and with a holistic & sustainable approach. Everything involved in the process is thoughtfully curated to create the healthiest and cleanest herbs that can be used many ways, from cooking to creating herbal remedies. You can find Meg selling her herbs and providing tasty samples at pop up markets around town at local shops or restaurants as well as rotating farmer's markets.
